01

What we are proposing

Ek hi platform jo scooter factory ka pura business chalata hai — purchase, assembly, quality, inventory aur dispatch.

Hum ek single ERP platform propose kar rahe hain jo aapke scooter business ka pura lifecycle cover karta hai: material khareedna, receive karna, scooter assemble karna, quality check (PDI), ready stock banana, dealer orders lena aur dispatch karna. Khaas baat — ismein ek Assembly Module hai: jaise hi scooter banta hai, raw material automatic kat jaata hai, aur PDI pass hone par hi scooter sellable stock mein jaata hai. Saath mein auto procurement planning jo shortage pehle se bata deti hai, taaki production kabhi ruke nahi.
