01

What we are proposing

A single platform that runs the whole scooter business — purchase, assembly, quality, inventory and dispatch.

We propose a single ERP platform that covers the complete lifecycle of your EV scooter business: purchasing materials, receiving them, assembling scooters, quality inspection (PDI), building sellable stock, taking dealer orders and dispatching. The key highlight is a built-in Assembly Module: the moment a scooter is assembled, raw material is deducted automatically, and only after passing PDI does the scooter move into sellable stock. It also includes auto procurement planning that flags shortages in advance, so production never stops.

Auto Loop

Procurement Planning looks at the production plan, dealer orders, current stock and minimum stock level to calculate the shortage, then automatically suggests what and how much to buy — this connects directly to Material Purchase, so stock never runs out unexpectedly.
